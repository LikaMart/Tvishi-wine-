Tvishi wine

At Tvishi Valley Cellars, our story begins with a deep-rooted passion for the unique character of Tvishi wine. We take pride in offering an exceptional experience through our scenic vineyard tours, guided tastings, and exclusive events, where the delicate honeyed notes of Tvishi truly come to life. With a commitment to sustainability and an unwavering dedication to excellence, our premium Tvishi wines reflect everything we value—respect for tradition, the warmth of our land and climate, and a devotion to the art of winemaking. Explore our vineyards and savor the pure essence of what makes Tvishi extraordinary—wine crafted with love and care. Nestled in the heart of the Racha region, our vineyards benefit from rich mineral soils and a distinctive microclimate that allows the local Tsolikauri grape to reach its fullest expression. Here, gentle mountain breezes, abundant sunlight, and pristine river valleys create the ideal environment for cultivating the naturally semi-sweet character that defines Tvishi. Our winemaking process honors both heritage and innovation: grapes are handpicked at peak ripeness, fermented with careful temperature control, and refined using techniques that preserve their natural aromatics. Visitors to Tvishi Valley Cellars are invited to immerse themselves in this harmony between land and craft. From walking through our sunlit terraces to tasting limited-edition vintages available exclusively at the estate, every moment is designed to reveal the story behind each bottle. Our team of passionate winemakers and hosts ensures that every guest experiences not only the flavor of Tvishi but also the history, culture, and dedication that shape it. Whether you are discovering Tvishi for the first time or returning to its familiar charm, we welcome you to share in a wine journey that captures the soul of our region.

Crafting Exquisite Wines

At Tvishi Heritage Estate, our story begins with the ancient winemaking traditions of Racha and the timeless elegance of Tvishi wine. Grown exclusively in the small Tvishi appellation, this naturally semi-sweet white wine is renowned for its floral aromas, honeyed notes, and vibrant acidity. We are honored to share this heritage through authentic vineyard experiences, tastings, and cultural events. Guided by sustainable practices and a deep respect for Georgian winemaking, our Tvishi wines are a living reflection of history, land, and craftsmanship. Visit us and discover the true soul of Tvishi — a wine born from tradition and perfected with care.
